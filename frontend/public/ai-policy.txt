AI AND DATA SCRAPING POLICY
===========================

Last Updated: March 2026

1. PROHIBITED USES:
The ASPPIBRA-DAO expressly prohibits the use of its data, text, images, code, and smart-contract ABIs published on this domain for the purposes of training, fine-tuning, or developing Artificial Intelligence (AI) models, including but not limited to Large Language Models (LLMs), without prior written authorization.

2. BOT ACCESS:
Crawling by search engine bots (Googlebot, Bingbot) for standard web indexing is permitted as per the /robots.txt directives. Scraping bots designed to mass-harvest data for AI corporative databases (e.g., GPTBot, CCBot, Anthropic-ai) are strictly forbidden.

3. CONTACT:
To request a commercial data licensing agreement, contact: legal@asppibra-dao.org.
